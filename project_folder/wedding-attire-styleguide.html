Attire Styleguide

Groomsmen

Do

  • Wear a bowtie
  • Acessorize with gold
  • Bring cufflinks
  • Get your suit tailored
  • (Optional) Wear a cummberbund which matches your tie
  • Wear a green dress coat and matching pants
  • Wear a white dress shirt

Don't

  • Wear an apron or bolo tie
  • Bring silver accents
  • Use colors not in the provided palette

Bridesmaids

Do

  • Choose a dress cut that suits you
  • Wear a floor length dress (shorter dresses can be worn but floor length is preferable)
  • Wear fun and unique acessories in gold with complementing palette colors
  • Embrace ruffles, petticoats, and frocks

Don't

  • Wear a dress which falls higher than the knees
  • Wear silver accents
